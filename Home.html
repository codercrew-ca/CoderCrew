A paragraph (from the Ancient Greek παράγραφος
, parágraphos, "to write beside") is a self-contained un
it of discourse in writing dealing with a particular po
int or idea. A paragraph consists of one or more sent
ences. Though not required by the syntax of any lan
guage, paragraphs are usually an expected part of f
ormal writing, used to organize longer prose.
