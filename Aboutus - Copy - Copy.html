We believe in the power of dreams, the joy of fulfilment and the legacy of success. And providing
children the knowledge to work towards fulfilling theirs, is what we are all about

A group of like minded parents, with several years of experience in the field of Information
technology have teamed up to be the wind beneath the wings of little aspirants to help them
have a goal, work towards it and see them conquer it

It’s always a new beginning, evolving as we go, embracing the latest in technology and assuring
that what the child learns today will be a strong foundation for his tomorrow
At Coder Crew we aim to provide the best learning experience to a child, of the latest technology, helping them discover the numerous possibilities of problem solving using this technology and channelizing their abilities to create a working model of the solution.
We aim to….
· empower them with the ability of logical and critical thinking which would trump all academic skills going forward.
· provide an in-depth knowledge of technology starting with the basics which ensures a strong foundation to build on.
· give children the power of technology to bring their dreams to life and to awaken little minds to the numerous possibilities of problem solving.
We believe that bringing in the fun element into learning helps retain their interest and motivates them to explore beyond their lessons.
After all, Education is about helping children discover their ideas, giving them the tools to build and preparing them to launch themselves into the world of infinite possibilities
